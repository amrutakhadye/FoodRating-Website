Where to find Christmas Cookies around Boston

Last week we took a galnce at some Boston restaurants that are perfect places to enjoy your Christmas meal. But if you want to eat at home, you may want to take some of the prep work off your plate. Christmas Cookies is an especially easy part to outsource - so here are our picks for local restaurants and cafes that are still offering take-home Cookies.

Boston Common Coffee Company

It's new North End of Boston was just included in our look at Boston's hottest bakeries, but you'll want to swing by the Washington Avenue location to pick up orders (placed by December 23) of cider pecan pie ($18; serves 6-8). Other take-home desserts are available too, including caramel apple cake ($24) and pumpkin cheesecake ($24).
