About Us

Food Maniac is a tribe of those who love food. It reviews restaurants with many tried, tested and tasted recipes and food columns. Our facebook groups and pages have a loyal following of over 20,000 of the most ardent foodies, from all corners of world.Food Maniac is increasingly becoming popular with the food lovers all over the world, ranging from Northern America to Europe, the Middle East, the Far East, Australia and New Zealand .

The Food Maniac is a not-for-profit operation. It is a result of the passion of many committed food lovers. Any profits made by us are donated to further the cause of promoting and show-casing the depth and diversity of the unique cuisine of the world
