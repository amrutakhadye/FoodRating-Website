Jimmy Peter reviewed Craigie On Main

When Craigie On Main first opened it was phenomenal, the food and service were worth the menu prices. The food and service have since gone down hill. We went on a Saturday evening and it was empty. Teresa Jack reviewed Vinoteca Di Monica

This is a great new spot. I've been here twice already and both times walked away completely satisfied. There is nothing better than a hot lobster roll in the summer!Liana is a charming host and a magnificent chef. Her specials are worth trying and her plum cake in season reminds me of my late mother's cherised recipe. Her pasta is perfect -- try the paccheri for a treat. This is a place to bring special friends and guests.
